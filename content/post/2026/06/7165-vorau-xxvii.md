Stift Vorau was the mighty monastery we've just seen. It is outside Vorau proper. Now we are in Vorau's own church,
Sankt Ägydius or Saint Giles, one of the most unusual churches I've ever seen, with two connected rooms. The concept is that of a medieval rood screen, but rood screens are uncommon in baroque.
