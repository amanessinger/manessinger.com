Bright sun, moderate contrast: why did I use DxO for that image?

Turns out, noise reduction is not the only feature of DxO. Better distortion correction is the other, and at 12 mm it
gave me a slightly bigger visible area. I needed it, because the precise join of lines in the lower right corner was cut
off in the original image. Sometimes sloppy framing can be fixed :grin:
